WELCOME TO HOTEL SAMIR

SAMIR – Residential Hotel feature’s 35 luxurious bedrooms with modern and stylish amenities. Situated in the most prestigious location of the city in MADARIPUR, Green Road signals, Behind Health & Hope Hospital near to the downtown business district, in a posh residential and commercial area surrounded by a good number of specialized modern Hospitals, Specially Square Hospital, BRB Hospital, Basundhara City mall, Universities, Schools and Colleges. It is in the center point of the capital city with excellent communication network. Be it leisure or business, expect absolute comfort with a touch of luxury. Enjoy beautiful homely environment, expect gracious accommodation and personalized service.
